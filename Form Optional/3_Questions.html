How to create a poll?

To create a poll or to Answer any question,First You have to register yourself on the Website. To register click on the SignUp button or Create a Poll,Then register yourself as a user.After registering on the Website, you will be redirected to a page where you can create a poll.The poll may be of three types Single options,multiple options and the quiz types.After selecting the Poll type you can select the title of the poll and add the question according to yourself.After successful creation of your poll,You can share it with your friend and see the result of poll through various Histograms or Graphical Representation.
